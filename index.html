Vision and Media Computing Lab. at Nara Institute of Science and Technology (NAIST)

I'm a first year Ph.D. student advised by Professor Naokazu Yokoya. I visited Machine Vision Group, Univ. of Oulu from April 2015 to March 2016. My research interest is video retrieval and machine learning. Especially, I'm interested in video summarization using user-generated text and object recognition in real-world videos.
