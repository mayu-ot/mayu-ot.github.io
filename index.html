I'm a third year Ph.D. student advised by Professor Naokazu Yokoya and Professor Kiyoshi Kiyokawa. I'm also a visiting student at Institute for Datability Science, Osaka University. I visited Machine Vision Group, Univ. of Oulu from April 2015 to March 2016. My research interest is video understanding and machine learning. Especially, I'm interested in video summarization using user-generated text.
